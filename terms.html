Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you participate in our tournaments:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number
  • Payment Information: PAN card details for prize distribution (as required by Indian tax laws)
  • Gameplay Data: Match history, scores, and device information
  • KYC Documents: For winners receiving prizes above ₹10,000

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Tournament registration and verification
  • Prize distribution and tax compliance
  • Improving our gaming platform
  • Fraud prevention and security

3. Data Sharing

We share information with:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay/PayU) for transaction completion
  • Government authorities for tax compliance
  • No third-party advertisers

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your data
  • Ask for corrections
  • Delete your account (except data required for legal compliance)

5. Age Restrictions

Participants under 18 years need parental consent to enter paid tournaments.
