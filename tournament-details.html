About Tournament

The Village Warriors Cup is a premier Free Fire tournament that brings together the best squads from across India. This tournament is designed to test your skills, strategy, and teamwork in intense competitive matches.

With a significant prize pool and recognition in the gaming community, this tournament is your chance to prove yourself and take your gaming career to the next level.

Tournament Schedule

Tournament Rules

1. Team Composition

Each team must consist of exactly 4 players. No substitutes are allowed during the tournament.

2. Game Settings

All matches will be played in the Classic Squad mode with default settings. Custom rooms will be created by the tournament organizers.

3. Scoring System

Points will be awarded based on placement and kills. Each kill = 1 point. Placement points: 1st = 15, 2nd = 12, 3rd = 10, and so on.

4. Fair Play

Any form of cheating, hacking, or exploiting game bugs will result in immediate disqualification.

5. Communication

Teams must join the designated Discord server for tournament communication and updates.
